Welcome to the World of Graphic Design!

Graphic design combines art and technology to create useful, meaningful, and functional visual and textual content that successfully and efficiently conveys or enhances a message for the intended audience. The scope of graphic design is vast and covers a range of activities, including logo creation, advertising, branding, marketing, and user experience design.

In fact, almost everyone today practices some form of graphic design in their daily life — whether it is adding text to an image for social media or color-coding a spreadsheet for work. Basically, if you're creating visual content, you're practicing some form of graphic design.
